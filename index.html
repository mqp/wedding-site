Accomodations

The CAC is right in the middle of downtown, so there are a lot of hotels nearby you can stay at. We had trouble arranging block reservations or group discounts, though, because of a home Reds game that weekend. We recommend booking your hotel room early if you want a hotel downtown!

We were able to get a block at the 21c Museum Hotel in Cincinnati, which is right next door the Contemporary Art Center. They are a bit pricier than many other hotels downtown, though. The group rate they offered is $239 a night. To get this rate, book at this link and use group code BQWED18, or call 844-577-5542 and mention the Buckey and Quander wedding by April 18th. If the block runs out, let us know and we may be able to extend it.
